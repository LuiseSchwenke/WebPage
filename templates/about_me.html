MY LIFE IN SHORT

I am Luise Schwenke and 25 years old.
In 2022 I graduated in Food Engineering at University of Applied Sciences Neubrandenburg/ Germany.
My biggest interest has always been in the areas of genetics, molecular biology and scientific research. Therefore in 2020 next to my bachelors, I started working in the laboratory of the local hospital. My obligatory internship for the bachelors I did in an analytical laboratory for foods and medical products. Next to it, I worked in a clinical laboratory for human genetics. After finishing my bachelors, I started working in a biotechnology company, designing pharmaceutical products.
For higher performance and results, the laboratory work is getting atomized more and more, especially by robots, which awakened my interest in IT and computer sciences.
It fascinated me so much, that I started taking coding lessons, to get to a professional level. During my jobs I always had the most fun helping to atomize processes and work flows, mainly with MS Excel. By now I created my own projects, including web development and data science. I am now taking further courses, to deepen and further expand my knowledge and skills, especially in the area of data sciences.
Privately I like to spend my time with my horse and enjoy the nature.
During an exchange semester in brazil during my bachelors, I discovered my love for surfing, which I do, whenever it is possible. Unfortunately, Berlin and whole Germany, my place of living, cannot offer an ocean with surfable waves, so I travel to the beaches, whenever I can.
Besides that, I enjoy to read books, especially about psychology and sciences or code some handy applications.
